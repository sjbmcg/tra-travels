About

A place to remember where you've been

Travel Blog is a personal travel journal built around an interactive map. Click anywhere in the world to drop a pin, write about what happened, why you went, and what made it special. Your memories build up over time into a route that traces everywhere you've been.

Once you've added enough memories, a cinematic slideshow unlocks — Your Journey — letting you relive your travels photo by photo.

Everything is private to your account. No public feeds, no followers, no likes. Just your memories.

Features

  • Interactive world map — click to place memories
  • Reverse geocoding — location names filled in automatically
  • Photo uploads
  • Chronological route with animated arrows
  • Your Journey — cinematic photo slideshow
  • Private per-user blogs via Google sign-in

Built with

  • Node.js + Express
  • PostgreSQL
  • Leaflet.js + OpenStreetMap
  • Cloudinary
  • Google OAuth via Passport.js
  • Deployed on Railway
